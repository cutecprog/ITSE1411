Austin Community College offeres instruction to more than 65,000 people annually in a service area that includes all or parts of eight countries: Travis, Williamson, Hays, Bastrop, Caldwell, Blanco, Gillespie, and Guadalupe. As a community college, ACC provides the region a broad scope of higher education programs and support services, with an emphasis on student outreach and student success.

ACC's workforce education programs prepare students to enter the workforce right away. Credit and non-credit programs are offered in more than 70 career fields. Academic transfer programs offer students two-year associate degrees that transfer to four-year universities. Majors are available in 38 subjects. Foundation skills programs such as English as a Second Langauge, Adult Education, and GED Preparation help people prepare for college or improve their job opportunities.
